Privacy Policy for RentBrief

Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

RentBrief is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • Property Data: Property details, tenant information, and financial transactions you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect, track, or transmit your usage patterns, analytics, or behavioral data.
  • Crash Data: If you opt in, anonymous crash reports may be sent through Apple's standard crash reporting system to help us improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the RentBrief app functionality:

  • Storing and organizing your property management data
  • Generating financial reports and summaries
  • Processing in-app purchases through Apple's App Store
  • Responding to support inquiries when you contact us

3. Data Storage and Security

All your property and financial data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's encrypted storage. We do not maintain any external servers or databases containing your personal information. Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features, including device encryption and Face ID/Touch ID.

4. Third-Party Services

RentBrief integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases and subscription management. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • Apple Mail: For sending support emails and sharing reports. No email content is stored by our app.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

5. Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can export your data at any time using the CSV export feature
  • You can delete individual records or all data by uninstalling the app
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

RentBrief is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing of your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
