## Description

RentBrief is the smart property management report generator designed for landlords and property managers who want clear, professional financial reports in seconds.

Whether you manage a single rental unit or a growing portfolio, RentBrief helps you track income, expenses, and maintenance — then turns it all into polished PDF reports ready for tax season, investors, or your own records.

KEY FEATURES

• Dashboard at a Glance — See total income, expenses, net profit, and occupancy rate for any month instantly.

• Property Management — Add properties with details like address, units, purchase price, and tenant info. Track occupancy status at a glance.

• Income & Expense Tracking — Log rent payments, maintenance costs, utilities, insurance, and more. Categorized and date-stamped for easy reference.

• Maintenance Orders — Create and track repair requests with priority levels and status updates. Never lose track of a work order again.

• Professional PDF Reports — Generate branded P&L statements, cash flow summaries, and property performance reports with one tap. Export or share instantly.

• CSV Export — Download all your transaction data as CSV for spreadsheet analysis or accountant handoff.

• Brand Customization (Pro) — Add your company name, logo, and custom footer text to every report. Present a professional image to clients and partners.

• Multi-Property Support — Manage up to 10 properties with Pro, or unlimited properties with Portfolio. Switch between properties effortlessly.

• Dark Mode — Full support for light and dark appearance. Easy on the eyes during late-night bookkeeping.
