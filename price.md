## Free Tier
- 1 property
- Basic Dashboard
- Income/expense recording
- Simple P&L report (PDF preview with watermark)
- CSV data export
- No brand customization
- No batch reports
- No email sending

## Pro Tier ($9.99/mo or $79.99/yr)
- Up to 10 properties
- All 4 report templates
- PDF generation (no watermark)
- Brand customization (logo, colors, company name)
- Batch report generation
- One-tap email sending
- Maintenance photo records
- Widget home screen cards
- CSV data import

## Portfolio Tier ($19.99/mo or $159.99/yr)
- Unlimited properties
- All Pro features
- Cross-portfolio view
- Advanced chart analysis
- YTD comparison reports
- Auto monthly report reminders
- Owner preference template assignment
- Priority support
